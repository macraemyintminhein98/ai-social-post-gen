# ScribbleFlow AI Social Post Generator

ScribbleFlow is a simple web application designed to help small business owners, freelancers, and content creators quickly generate engaging social media posts. Just provide a topic or keywords, and our AI will suggest 3-5 post options for platforms like Twitter, LinkedIn, and Facebook.

## Features

*   **Quick Generation**: Get multiple post ideas instantly.
*   **AI-Powered**: Leverages advanced language models for creative suggestions.
*   **User-Friendly**: Minimalist interface focused on core functionality.

## How to Use

1.  Enter your desired topic or keywords into the input field.
2.  Click 'Generate Posts'.
3.  Receive 3-5 unique social media post options.
4.  Consider upgrading for unlimited generations and advanced features.
